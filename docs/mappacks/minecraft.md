Minecraft is a platformer mappack based on Minecraft and released for Alesan99's Entities, Originally released on the 3rd of June 2018. The mappack is really poorly made and has the strangest story to it where I claim that Mario is Steve's father.

## Credits

The original tileset was based on a tileset in Glitchy Error by [numlock09azz](https://forum.stabyourself.net/memberlist.php?mode=viewprofile&u=2327) ([Original Post](https://forum.stabyourself.net/viewtopic.php?f=12&t=2913))
