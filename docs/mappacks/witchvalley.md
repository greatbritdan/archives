Witch Valley is a platformer mappack based on a map by Alesan99 called "The Witch Village" and released for Alesan99's Entities, Originally released on the 19th of August, 2019. This mappack is odd as it's the only mappack ever to be based on a mappack I never made and likly i will never do something like this again, it's decent at best with some really annoying sections.
