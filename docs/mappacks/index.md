# Mappacks

Welcome to home of Britdan's Mappacks!

All maps, new and old will be hosted here and available to download. All mappacks going forward will still be released on the Stabyourself.net forum but downloads will come directly from here.

## Why?

I want to have more control in hosting my own content, while the forum is unlikly to go in the time I'll still be involved in the community, having this put together now will reduce the workload if it were to happen. Having my content here also allows me to do something I've always wanted to do, post unreleased and unfinished mappacks.

## What to expect

What you can expect is the same sort of information I'd lay out normally but with some added benifits, most importantly I'm going back and updating mappacks to work with the lastest version of Alesan99's Entities. Not changing the content unless a feature has spesificly been removed.
