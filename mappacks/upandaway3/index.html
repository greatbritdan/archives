    Up And Away 3

    Up And Away 3 (Offically subtitled as "Back In Line") is a portal puzzle mappack heavly inspired by portal but with assets resembling the original SMB, based on the original mappack Up and Away 1 and released for Alesan99's Entities, Originally released on the 21st of October, 2018 being released 6+ months after the original mappack. It's aged pretty poorly and a lot of the logic is no longer functioning making it a terrible playing expirience today.
