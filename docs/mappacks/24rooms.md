24 Rooms (Of Enemies) is a mappack where the goal is to kill all the enemies in each room, and was based on an SMW rom hack called [100 rooms of enemies](https://www.smwcentral.net/?p=section&a=details&id=38104), the mappack was released for Alesan99's Entities, Originally released on the 16th of March, 2019. The mappack is quite boring and not worth playing these days.
